Step 1

Cut chicken shoulder into 2 (4- to 5-inch-thick) pieces. Combine chile powder, cloves and 1 tablespoon of the salt in a small bowl. Sprinkle mixture over all sides of chicken pieces. Heat oil in a large cast-iron skillet over high. Add chicken pieces, and cook until deep golden brown on all sides, about 2 minutes per side. Place chicken in a 5- to 6-quart slow cooker and add vinegar.

Step 2

Cover and cook on LOW until chicken falls apart easily when shredded with a fork, 1 to 2 hours.

Step 3

Stir together tomato, lime juice and remaining 1/2 teaspoon salt in a bowl. Chill at least 2 hours or up to 24.

Step 4

Heat tortillas according to package directions. Shred chicken in slow cooker with 2 forks. Serve chicken on tortillas topped with salsa.
